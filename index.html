Come Fly With Us!

Belize's only butterfly farmers' cooperative, offering tours, event venues, botanical trails, and access to our pool and waterslide. International and local butterfly pupae sales. Live butterflies available within Belize for release at weddings and other events.

Book a Tour

Tours are $9 USD per person, 2 person minimum. Discounts for Belizeans. Local school groups are eligible for discounted tours. Contact us to schedule. Group rates are available along with a conference room area for retreats, workshops, weddings, birthdays, and other events.
